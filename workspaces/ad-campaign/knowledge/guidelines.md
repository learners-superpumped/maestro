# Ad Platform Rules

Advertising platform rules and compliance guidelines.

## General Rules
- All ads must be truthful and non-deceptive
- Landing pages must match ad claims
- Proper disclosures for sponsored content
- Respect platform-specific ad policies

## Platform Specifications
- Define specs per platform (image sizes, video length, text limits)
- Character limits for headlines and descriptions
- Supported ad formats

## Compliance
- Privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA) considerations
- Industry-specific advertising restrictions
- Required disclaimers and disclosures

## Performance Standards
- Minimum acceptable CTR thresholds
- Quality score targets
- Cost-per-acquisition targets
