# X Platform Guidelines

Platform-specific rules and best practices for X (Twitter).

## Format
- Single tweet: 280 characters max
- Thread: Multiple tweets connected in sequence
- Images: Up to 4 per tweet (1200x675px recommended)
- Videos: Up to 2 min 20 sec
- Hashtags: 2-3 per tweet for optimal reach

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do: Be concise and impactful
- Do: Use threads for longer narratives
- Do: Engage with replies and mentions
- Don't: Spam hashtags (reduces engagement)
- Don't: Use clickbait or misleading content
- Don't: Post duplicate content across tweets
